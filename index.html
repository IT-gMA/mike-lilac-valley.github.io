Lilac Valley Farm Stay is a simple, rustic farm stay accommodation in the hills of the Blue Mountains. The property is owned by Marina Ye, a local interior designer who purchased the old rundown barn three years ago with a plan to renovate.

About us

After years of hard work, the property now features a beautifully restored, open plan, luxury barn house, on four acres of land home to sheep, chickens, ducks and one very friendly goat named Rosie. There is an outdoor plunge pool just off the back verandah, and a stunning cottage garden. The Lilac Valley Farm Stay has been incredibly popular on AirBnB, and was even featured in a handful of online articles
